info Overview

Welcome to CityNav! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our navigation and mapping services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

shield Our Commitment
We believe that privacy is a fundamental right. We minimize data collection, encrypt sensitive information, and never sell your personal data to third parties.

By using CityNav, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.

data_usage What Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

location_on Location Data
Real-time location, search history, frequently visited places, and route preferences to provide navigation services.
account_circle Account Information
Name, email address, profile picture, and preferences when you create an account with us.
devices Device Information
Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
analytics Usage Data
App interactions, feature usage, crash reports, and performance data to improve our services.
location_off Location Data Notice
Location access is essential for navigation services. You can control location permissions in your device settings, but this may limit app functionality.

settings How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Navigation Services: Provide turn-by-turn directions, traffic updates, and route optimization
  • Personalization: Customize your experience based on preferences and usage patterns
  • Safety Features: Enable emergency services, speed alerts, and safety notifications
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and fix issues
  • Communication: Send important updates, safety alerts, and service notifications
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and legal requests
psychology Machine Learning
We use anonymized and aggregated data to improve our routing algorithms and predict traffic patterns. This helps provide better navigation for all users.

share Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Emergency Services: Location data may be shared with emergency responders when you use SOS features
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to sharing with third parties
verified_user Third-Party Services
All third-party partners are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the specified purposes. We regularly audit their compliance.

my_location Location Data Handling

Location data is crucial for navigation services. Here's how we handle it:

  • Real-time Processing: Location data is processed in real-time for navigation and is not stored permanently on our servers
  • Route History: Recent routes are stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time
  • Anonymization: Historical location data is anonymized and aggregated for traffic analysis
  • Retention: Precise location data is only retained for 30 days, then anonymized or deleted
  • Control: You can pause location tracking, delete location history, and export your data at any time
gps_off Location Controls
Visit Settings > Privacy > Location Data to manage your location preferences, view stored data, and delete location history.

security Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256
  • Access Control: Strict access controls limit who can view your personal information
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing are conducted regularly
  • Incident Response: We have procedures in place to respond to and notify users of security incidents
  • Data Minimization: We only collect and retain data necessary for service operation
report_problem Report Security Issues
If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to security@citynav.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure.

gavel Your Privacy Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (right to be forgotten)
  • Portability: Export your data in a commonly used format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your personal information
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for processing at any time

To exercise these rights, visit your account settings or contact us directly. We will respond within 30 days.
